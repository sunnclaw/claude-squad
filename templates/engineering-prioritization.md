# Engineering Prioritization

Score each item from 1 to 5.

## Criteria

- Revenue impact
- Customer evidence strength
- Time to ship
- Confidence
- Strategic leverage

## Penalties

- Platform complexity
- Ongoing maintenance burden
- Unclear success metric

## Formula

`(Revenue impact + Customer evidence strength + Time to ship + Confidence + Strategic leverage) - (Platform complexity + Ongoing maintenance burden + Unclear success metric)`

## Decision

- 10 or higher: do now
- 6 to 9: backlog candidate
- 5 or lower: reject or re-scope

# Weekly Review

## 1. Revenue Reality

- What closed, expanded, or churned this week?
- What did customers ask for repeatedly?
- Where did deals or onboarding stall?

## 2. Scorecard Review

- Which number matters most right now?
- What changed?
- What did not change despite effort?

## 3. Engineering Review

- What shipped?
- What is still too large and needs to be cut down?
- What work should stop immediately?

## 4. Decision For Next Week

State one company-level bet:

`Next week we will focus engineering on:`

## 5. Cleanup

- Move unfinished but still valuable work to `Next`.
- Move weak ideas to `Later`.
- Delete backlog items with no metric or owner.

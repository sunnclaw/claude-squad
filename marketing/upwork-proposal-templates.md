## Customization Guide

### Always Customize
1. **[Client Name]** - Use their actual name or company name
2. **[specific need]** - Reference their exact problem
3. **[X] days** - Realistic timeline estimate
4. **$[Y]** - Competitive but fair pricing

### Research Before Proposing
1. Check client's hiring history
2. Look at their company website
3. Read job description twice
4. Note any specific requirements

### Pricing Guidelines

| Project Type | Entry Price | Pro Price |
|--------------|-------------|-----------|
| Simple Zap (1-2 zaps) | $150-300 | $300-500 |
| Medium automation | $500-1,000 | $1,000-2,000 |
| Complex system | $1,500-3,000 | $3,000-5,000 |
| Enterprise | $5,000+ | $10,000+ |

### Proposal Success Tips
1. **Respond quickly** - Within 12 hours of posting
2. **Be specific** - Reference their exact need
3. **Show proof** - Mention relevant results
4. **Ask a question** - Encourage response
5. **Keep it short** - 150-200 words max

## Master Script Templates

### Front-Desk Opener

```
Hi, this is [Your Name]. I'm doing brief research with local dental practices
about missed inbound calls and after-hours appointment requests.

Who is the best person to speak with about front-desk operations or
new patient scheduling?
```

### If Asked for Context

```
I'm validating a workflow that helps clinics recover appointments when calls
are missed during busy periods. I only need 10 to 15 minutes with [OWNER NAME]
or the practice manager to understand the current process.
```

### Gatekeeper Asks for Email

```
Happy to. What is the best email address to reach [OWNER NAME] directly?
I'll mention this conversation so it's not mistaken for generic vendor outreach.
```

### Voicemail (if no answer)

```
Hi [OWNER NAME], this is [Your Name]. I'm speaking with dental clinics about
missed inbound calls and how many appointments slip when the front desk is
overloaded or the office is closed.

I have a short idea for missed-call recovery and I'd value 10 to 15 minutes
of feedback. You can call me back at [Your Number], or I'll send a short
email as well. Thank you.
```

## Post-Call Actions

### If You Get an Email Address

1. Record it in the tracker
2. Update `dental-clinic-outbound-tracker.csv` with the new email
3. Send the follow-up email template

### Follow-Up Email Template (After Getting Email)

```
Subject: Quick question about missed new-patient calls

Hi [First Name],

Thank you for speaking with me today. As promised, I wanted to follow up
with more details.

I'm working with private dental clinics to understand how they handle missed
inbound calls and after-hours appointment requests.

Several practices have told me they lose bookings when the front desk is busy,
lunch coverage is thin, or calls come in after hours. I'm testing a lightweight
missed-call recovery workflow that texts and routes follow-up fast enough to
save more appointments without adding staff.

Would you be open to a 15-minute conversation this week? I'm not selling a
full platform on the call. I'm trying to understand:

- How often calls go unanswered
- What happens to those leads today
- What a recovered appointment would be worth

If it's easier, reply with a time and best direct number.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

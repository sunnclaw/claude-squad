# 30-Day Revenue System

Window: 2026-03-10 to 2026-04-09
Owner: CTO

## Goal

Get to the first repeatable paid orders without building infrastructure the company does not yet need.

## Success Criteria

By 2026-04-09:
- Payment method is usable
- 40 qualified leads contacted
- 8 quotes sent
- 2 paid orders closed
- 2 case studies or testimonials published

## Execution Rule

At any moment, there can be:
- 1 engineering priority
- 1 sales priority
- 1 delivery improvement priority

Everything else stays in backlog.

## Now

### Revenue-critical build queue

| Priority | Item | Owner | Due date | Metric |
|------|------|------|------|------|
| P0 | Make payment collection operational on site and in order flow | CTO | 2026-03-12 | Paid orders can be completed |
| P0 | Turn leads, quotes, and paid status into one weekly dashboard | CTO | 2026-03-12 | No lead goes unfollowed |
| P1 | Create standard quote and proposal package for the 3 core offers | CTO | 2026-03-13 | Quotes sent within 30 minutes |
| P1 | Create delivery kits for content, code review, and research | CTO | 2026-03-15 | Delivery cycle time under 3 days |
| P1 | Publish 2 proof assets tied to offers, not generic content | CTO | 2026-03-17 | Better quote conversion |

### Sales execution queue

| Priority | Item | Cadence |
|------|------|------|
| P0 | Direct outreach to warm contacts | Daily |
| P0 | Follow-up on every open conversation within 24 hours | Daily |
| P1 | Publish offer-driven content with CTA to order page | 3x per week |
| P1 | Ask every completed customer for testimonial and referral | After each delivery |

## Not Now

These are explicitly deprioritized until 2 paid orders exist:

- New service categories
- Complex CRM
- Backend order management
- Multi-user systems
- Analytics pipelines
- Additional agent hiring for speculative work
- Major website redesign
- SEO work not connected to an offer page

## Weekly Review Template

### Week 1

- Is payment working end-to-end?
- How many leads were generated?
- Were quotes sent within 24 hours?
- What blocked conversion?

### Week 2

- Which offer is easiest to sell?
- Which channel produced the most real conversations?
- What part of delivery created drag?

### Week 3

- Which manual steps repeated 3+ times?
- What can be templated without building software?
- Are prices too low for the effort involved?

### Week 4

- What should be doubled down on?
- What should be deleted?
- What is the next constraint after the first paid orders?

## Backlog Scoring

Score every idea from 1 to 5 in each column.

| Item | Revenue in 30 days | Time to ship | Repeat use | Confidence | Total |
|------|------|------|------|------|------|
| Example: payment instructions on website | 5 | 5 | 4 | 5 | 19 |

Rules:
- Ship items scoring 16+
- Challenge items scoring 12-15
- Reject or defer anything below 12

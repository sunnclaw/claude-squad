# Revenue Roadmap

This is the only list of approved priorities. Maximum 3 active items.

## Prioritization formula

Score each item from 1-5 in each category:

- Revenue impact
- Customer urgency
- Confidence from evidence
- Speed to first proof
- Strategic pull

Subtract:

- Delivery complexity
- Operational burden

`priority score = impact + urgency + confidence + speed + strategic pull - complexity - ops burden`

If two items score similarly, choose the one that gets customer evidence sooner.

## Anti-overbuilding checklist

Before approving a technical initiative, confirm:

- We have evidence from customers, sales, support, or operations.
- A simpler manual or low-code version is not enough.
- The work shortens a bottleneck that exists today.
- We know how success will be measured within 2-4 weeks.
- We are willing to delete or replace this solution later.

# Weekly Revenue Review

Run this once per week. Time box to 45 minutes.

## Inputs

- current pipeline and recently closed deals
- product funnel metrics
- customer complaints blocking conversion or retention
- incidents and support load
- engineering cycle time

## Review Questions

1. What shipped last week changed revenue, conversion, onboarding speed, or retention?
2. What work consumed time but did not move a business metric?
3. Which customer problem is closest to cash right now?
4. What should stop immediately?
5. What can remain manual for another month?
6. What is the single highest-value engineering slice for next week?

## Output

- one `Revenue Now` priority
- one `Delivery Capacity` priority, if needed
- zero or one `Exploration` bet with a firm expiry date
- explicit kills / deferrals

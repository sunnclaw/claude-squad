# Build vs Buy vs Manual

Use this before any non-trivial technical investment.

## Fast Decision Table

Choose `manual` if the answer is yes to most of these:

- Is the volume still low?
- Is the workflow still changing weekly?
- Is the main need learning rather than scale?
- Can a human do it in less than 30 minutes per occurrence?

Choose `buy` if the answer is yes to most of these:

- Is this a commodity capability?
- Can a vendor solve 80 percent of the need quickly?
- Is the integration surface small?
- Is the pricing acceptable relative to current revenue?

Choose `build` if the answer is yes to most of these:

- Does this directly affect conversion, retention, or delivery margin?
- Is the workflow unique to the business?
- Has the process repeated enough that the shape is now stable?
- Will owning it create leverage in sales or operations?

## Approval Thresholds

- Less than 1 day: team can decide locally.
- 1 to 5 days: requires a one-pager.
- More than 5 days: requires explicit CTO approval and kill criteria.
- More than 2 weeks: split into phases that each deliver measurable learning.

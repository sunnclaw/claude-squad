# Operating Cadence

## Weekly Rhythm

### 1. Revenue Review

Questions:

+ Which deals are blocked by missing product capability?
+ Which current users are showing expansion or churn signals?
+ Which support issues are recurring often enough to deserve product work?
+ What did we ship last week that changed a KPI?

Decisions:

+ top 1 weekly goal
+ top 3 initiatives
+ explicit non-goals

### 2. Execution Review

Questions:

+ Is each active item tied to a metric?
+ Is any item larger than one week to first customer value?
+ Are we carrying too many partially complete tasks?

Decisions:

+ cut scope
+ kill low-leverage work
+ reassign ownership

### 3. Ship Review

Questions:

+ What reached production?
+ What reached customers?
+ What changed in pipeline, conversion, retention, or efficiency?

Decisions:

+ keep investing
+ iterate
+ stop

## Monthly Rhythm

1. Review the last four weeks of scorecards.
2. Identify repeated customer asks and repeated operational pain.
3. Remove projects with weak evidence.
4. Re-rank the roadmap around the current revenue path.

## Quarterly Rhythm

1. Reconfirm the business model.
2. Reconfirm the core product wedge.
3. Review whether architecture still matches company stage.
4. Decide the next constraint to remove: acquisition, activation, retention, expansion, or ops.

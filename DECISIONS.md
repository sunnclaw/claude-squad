# Decisions

## 2026-03-11 - Pivot to email-first outreach strategy

- **Context:** Phone calls require human execution. Sales Manager AI agent has prepared all scripts and targets but cannot make real phone calls. Week 1 targets: 25 clinics contacted, 15 conversations, 5 qualified leads. Current status: 0 calls.
- **Decision:** Pivot to email-first outreach. Sales Manager will execute email outreach autonomously using prepared templates. Phone follow-up remains an option when human resources become available.
- **Why now:** Revenue pipeline is blocked. Email-only is executable by AI agents immediately.
- **Alternatives rejected:**
  - Wait for human (indefinite delay)
  - Hire human sales rep (costly, slow)
- **Revenue link:** Direct - email outreach generates leads which flow to quotes and orders
- **Condition to revisit:** If email conversion rates are significantly lower than expected after 2 weeks, reconsider phone-first with human execution
- **Review date:** 2026-03-25

## 2026-03-10 - Website UI redesign deferred to Week 2+

- Context: External request (CLA-21) to form development team for website UI optimization
- Decision: Defer to "Later" lane. Current Week 1 focus is dental clinic outreach for revenue. Website is functional and does not block sales.
- Why now: Per Decision Filter, website redesign is "mostly about elegance" and does not directly close deals or speed delivery.
- Revenue link: None - website is not blocking qualified leads from contacting us
- Condition to revisit: After Week 1 revenue targets met (5 qualified leads), OR if a qualified lead specifically cites website as blocker
- Review date: 2026-03-17 (end of Week 1)

## 2026-03-10 - Revenue-first technical operating system

- Context: the company needs a technical system that increases revenue quickly, keeps engineering focused, and avoids overbuilding.
- Decision: adopt a simple operating model built around one business constraint, strict work lanes, small WIP limits, and evidence-based prioritization.
- Why now: a one-person company pays a large tax for unclear priorities and speculative engineering.
- Alternatives rejected: ad hoc prioritization, roadmap-by-ideas, platform-first architecture.
- Review date: 2026-04-10

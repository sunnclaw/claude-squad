# Delivery Cadence

## Weekly Cycle

### Monday

- Review scoreboard
- Identify the single biggest business bottleneck
- Commit to one main engineering outcome for the week

### Tuesday to Thursday

- Ship small increments daily
- Review metrics and customer feedback continuously
- Escalate blockers within the same day

### Friday

- Demo shipped work
- Review outcome vs metric
- Write down what to continue, stop, or simplify

## Work-In-Progress Limits

- One company priority at a time
- Maximum two active engineering projects per engineer
- No side quests without explicit tradeoff approval

## Definition Of Done

Work is not done until:

- deployed
- monitored
- documented at the surface level
- measurable
- handed off to support or operations if needed

## Escalation Rules

Escalate immediately if:

- checkout is broken
- sign-up is broken
- production data integrity is at risk
- support volume spikes due to product regressions
- an incident affects paying customers

## Product-Engineering Loop

For every material feature:

1. define the user problem
2. define the metric
3. ship the smallest version
4. observe behavior
5. decide to expand, simplify, or kill

Do not skip step 5.

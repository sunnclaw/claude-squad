Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI sound on the phone?
Our AI uses advanced natural language processing to sound professional and conversational. Patients often can't tell they're speaking with an AI system. The voice is natural, friendly, and professionally trained for dental clinic interactions.
What phone systems do you integrate with?
We integrate with all major dental practice management systems including Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, Curve Dental, and cloud-based systems. Setup typically takes less than 24 hours with no technical expertise required.
What if a patient has complex questions?
The AI is trained to handle common dental inquiries (appointments, insurance, hours, services). For complex questions, it will schedule a callback from your staff at a convenient time or offer to transfer to your office during business hours.
How does the 30-day guarantee work?
If you don't recover enough patients to cover the service cost within 30 days, we'll refund your first month in full. No questions asked. We're confident in our service because it works.
Can I customize what the AI says?
Absolutely. We work with you to customize the greeting, questions asked, and overall script to match your clinic's voice and specific needs. You have full control over the patient experience.
What about HIPAA compliance?
Our system is fully HIPAA compliant. All patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We sign Business Associate Agreements (BAA) and maintain strict security protocols to protect patient information.

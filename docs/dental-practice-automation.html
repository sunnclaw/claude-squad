Frequently Asked Questions

How does the missed call recovery work?

When a call goes unanswered, our system automatically sends a text within 30 seconds, asking how we can help. Patients can reply to schedule, ask questions, or request a callback.

Will patients know it's automated?

Our texts feel personal and conversational. Most patients appreciate the quick response and don't realize it's automated.

What phone systems do you integrate with?

We work with all major dental phone systems including Weave, Patient Prism, and traditional PBX systems. If we don't have an integration, we'll build one.

What's included in the free trial?

Full access to all features for 14 days. No credit card required. We'll show you exactly how many appointments we recover.

Case Study: How One Dental Practice Recovered $2,000/Month in Missed Appointments

Published: March 11, 2026 · Reading Time: 9 minutes

Dr. Sarah Chen's dental practice was losing patients every week without knowing it. After implementing an automated callback system, she recovered $2,000+ per month in previously lost appointments. Here's the complete story.

Case Study at a Glance

  • Practice: Chen Family Dentistry, Austin TX
  • Size: 1 dentist, 3 staff, 12 operatories
  • Challenge: Unknown number of missed calls and lost appointments
  • Solution: Automated missed call recovery system
  • Result: $2,000+/month in recovered revenue

The Background

Practice Profile

Dr. Sarah Chen opened Chen Family Dentistry in 2019. By 2025, the practice had grown to:

  • 2,800 active patients
  • 18-22 new patients per month
  • 4 staff members (including Dr. Chen)
  • Average production: $95,000/month

The Problem

Despite steady growth, Dr. Chen noticed something concerning:

"We were getting busier, but I felt like we should be growing faster. New patient numbers were flat for 6 months despite increased marketing. I didn't understand why."

The practice was spending $1,500/month on Google Ads and local SEO, generating 40-50 calls monthly. But where were those leads going?

The Discovery

Phone Log Analysis

When Dr. Chen's office manager analyzed phone logs from the past 3 months, the findings were shocking:

Metric Data
Total calls received 1,247
Calls answered 1,056 (85%)
Calls missed 191 (15%)
Voicemails left 62 (32% of missed)
Missed calls with NO voicemail 129 (68% of missed)
The Hidden Problem: 129 callers never left voicemail. These were completely invisible to the practice. They called, got no answer, and hung up—likely calling a competitor.

When Were Calls Being Missed?

The timing analysis revealed clear patterns:

  • 12pm-1pm (lunch): 47 missed calls (25%)
  • 8am-9am (opening): 28 missed calls (15%)
  • 4pm-5pm (closing): 22 missed calls (12%)
  • Weekends: 34 missed calls (18%)

The Solution

Implementing Automated Callback

Dr. Chen implemented our missed call recovery system with the following configuration:

Setup (1 hour total)

  1. Connected phone system to callback platform
  2. Customized callback script for dental practice
  3. Set callback delay to 2 minutes
  4. Configured business hours vs. after-hours responses
  5. Integrated with practice scheduling software

How It Works

  • Missed call detected: System logs caller ID instantly
  • 2-minute delay: Brief pause prevents "pocket dial" callbacks
  • Automated call: System calls back with professional greeting
  • Schedule or message: Caller can schedule appointment or leave message
  • Notification: Staff receives instant alert with call details

Lessons Learned

What Dr. Chen Discovered

1. Most Missed Callers Don't Leave Voicemail

"I always assumed if someone really wanted to reach us, they'd leave a message. I was wrong. 68% of our missed calls had no voicemail. We were completely invisible to these patients."

2. Speed Matters More Than I Thought

"The callback system reaches people within 2 minutes. When we manually returned calls at the end of the day, conversion was almost zero. Immediate callback converts at 4x the rate."

3. It's Not Just New Patients

"I expected new patient calls, but I was surprised by how many existing patients we were missing. These are patients who might have gone elsewhere for their next cleaning."

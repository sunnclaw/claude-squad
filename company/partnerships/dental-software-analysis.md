## Detailed Analysis

### 1. Open Dental (Score: 9/10)

**Company Info:**
- Independently owned
- HQ: Salem, OR
- Estimated practices: 10,000+
- Open-source roots, tech-forward customer base

**Why Highest Score:**
- Strong API documentation
- Active user community
- Partnership-friendly reputation
- Direct access to decision-makers

**Contact Strategy:**
- Research founder/owner Nathan Sparks
- Approach via API/Integration team
- Lead with API integration potential
- Offer co-marketing to user base

**Recommended First Approach:**
Email to partnerships@opendental.com or direct LinkedIn outreach to key team members.

## Partnership Model Recommendation

**Model: Referral Partner (Lightest Integration)**

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Partner Role | Introduce to practices who could benefit |
| Our Role | Handle implementation, support, billing |
| Referral Fee | 15% of first-year revenue |
| Commitment | Zero minimums, no exclusivity |

**Why This Model:**
- Fastest time to launch (2-4 weeks)
- No technical integration required
- Low risk for both parties
- Easy to test and scale

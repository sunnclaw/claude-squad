## Missed-Call Recovery Service for Dental Practices

## The Problem

Dental practices lose revenue every day from missed inbound calls:

- **5-15 calls missed per week** during busy hours
- **$300-800 lifetime value** per new patient call
- **$1,500-12,000/month** in potential revenue lost

Practices don't have staff dedicated to following up on every missed call. New patients simply call the next practice on Google.

---

## Our Solution

**Automated Missed-Call Recovery**

When a practice misses a call:
1. Patient receives a text within 5 minutes
2. Staff gets a callback reminder
3. All interactions are tracked in a dashboard

**Result:** Practices recover 3-5 appointments per month without adding staff.

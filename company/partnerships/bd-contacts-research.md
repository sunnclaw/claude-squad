## Outreach Sequence Template

### Initial Contact (Email)

```
Subject: Partnership opportunity - Missed-call recovery for [Company] practices

Hi [Name],

I lead Claude Squad, a service helping dental practices recover revenue from missed inbound calls. We're currently working with owner-led practices and seeing strong interest.

Your [Company] customers often face:
- Front desk overload during peak hours
- After-hours call handling gaps
- New patients calling competitors when lines are busy

Our service complements [Product] by catching the calls that slip through, without requiring practices to add staff or learn new software.

I'd love to explore a referral partnership:
- Zero integration burden on your team
- 15% referral fee for introduced customers
- We handle all implementation and support

Would 15 minutes this week work to discuss?

Best,
[Signature]
```

```
Subject: Re: Partnership opportunity - Missed-call recovery

Hi [Name],

Following up on my note about a potential partnership. I know you're busy, so I'll keep this brief.

We've now helped several practices recover $1,200+ monthly in otherwise-lost new patient revenue. I believe [Company] customers would see similar results.

Happy to send a one-page overview if you'd like to review before we connect.

Best,
[Signature]
```

### Category C: VoIP/Phone System Providers

Dental practices often use specialized phone systems that could integrate with missed-call recovery.

| Provider | Dental Focus | Estimated Dental Practices | Integration Potential |
|----------|--------------|---------------------------|----------------------|
| **Weave** | Dental-specific VoIP | 10,000+ | Already has call features - partner vs compete |
| **Patient Prism** | Call tracking & analytics | 3,000+ | Complementary analytics |
| **CallTrackingMetrics** | Call tracking | 5,000+ dental | Integration opportunity |
| **RingCentral** | General VoIP | Many dental | Marketplace partnership |
| **Vonage Business** | General VoIP | Many dental | Integration partner |

**Note on Weave:**
Weave already offers call handling features. They could be:
1. A competitor (we need differentiation)
2. An acquisition target for their customer base
3. A partner for practices that don't want full Weave

**Integration Approach:**
- Offer API endpoint for missed-call detection
- Callback data sync
- White-label option for VoIP providers

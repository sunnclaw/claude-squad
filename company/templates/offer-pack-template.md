## Delivery Definition

```md
Done means:
- deliverables are sent
- QA checklist is complete
- revisions are either closed or clearly bounded
- testimonial or proof request is sent
```

## Reuse Rule

After each sale, update this offer pack with:
- objections that came up
- scoping mistakes
- phrases that improved conversion
- delivery steps that should become checklist items

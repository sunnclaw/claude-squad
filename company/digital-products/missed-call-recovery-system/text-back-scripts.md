# Text-Back Script Templates for Dental Practices

## 10 Proven Templates for Missed Call Recovery

Use these templates when responding to missed calls. Customize with your practice name and contact information.

---

## Template 1: Simple Callback Offer

**Use when:** Any missed call during business hours

```
Hi! This is [Practice Name]. We noticed we missed your call. We'd love to help you. Would you like us to call you back? Reply YES with your preferred time, or call us at [Phone Number]. Thanks!
```

---

## Template 2: New Patient Focus

**Use when:** Likely new patient call (unknown number)

```
Hi! Thanks for calling [Practice Name]. We're sorry we missed you! Are you looking to schedule an appointment? We have openings this week. Reply with your name and we'll call you back within the hour. Or call us at [Phone Number].
```

---

## Template 3: Emergency Acknowledgment

**Use when:** Call came outside normal hours or multiple calls in short time

```
Hi from [Practice Name]. We received your call. If this is a dental emergency, please call our emergency line at [Emergency Number]. Otherwise, we'll call you back first thing in the morning. Reply URGENT if you need immediate assistance.
```

---

## Template 4: Appointment Confirmation Follow-up

**Use when:** Call came around appointment times

```
Hi! [Practice Name] here. We missed your call. Are you calling about an upcoming appointment? Reply with your name and we'll confirm your details. Or call us at [Phone Number] during office hours.
```

---

## Template 5: Insurance Inquiry

**Use when:** Call came during insurance verification hours

```
Hi! [Practice Name] received your call. Are you inquiring about insurance or payment options? We accept most major insurance plans. Reply with your question and we'll call you back with answers. Or reach us at [Phone Number].
```

---

## Template 6: After-Hours Response

**Use when:** Call came after closing

```
Hi! This is [Practice Name]. We're closed for the day but received your call. We'll call you back when we open at [Open Time] tomorrow. If this is urgent, reply URGENT. Otherwise, have a great evening!
```

---

## Template 7: Weekend Response

**Use when:** Call came on Saturday/Sunday

```
Hi from [Practice Name]! We missed your call. We're closed on weekends but will call you first thing Monday morning. If you're experiencing a dental emergency, please call [Emergency Number]. Otherwise, see you Monday!
```

---

## Template 8: Multiple Missed Calls

**Use when:** Same number called 2+ times

```
Hi! [Practice Name] here. We see you've tried reaching us multiple times and we apologize for missing you. We want to help! Please reply with the best time to call back, and we'll make it a priority. Or call us now at [Phone Number].
```

---

## Template 9: Existing Patient Recognition

**Use when:** Number matches patient in system

```
Hi [Patient Name]! [Practice Name] here. We missed your call. Are you calling about your upcoming appointment, or do you need to schedule something new? Reply with how we can help, and we'll call you back within 30 minutes.
```

---

## Template 10: Generic Professional

**Use when:** Unsure of call purpose

```
Hi! This is [Practice Name]. We missed your call and want to make sure we connect. Please call us back at [Phone Number] or reply with your question and the best time to reach you. We're here to help!
```

---

## Best Practices

### Timing
- Send within 5 minutes of missed call when possible
- After-hours calls: Send immediately, schedule callback for next business day
- Weekend calls: Send immediately, offer emergency line if applicable

### Personalization
- Use patient name when available
- Reference last visit or upcoming appointment when possible
- Match tone to patient history (new vs. established)

### Call to Action
- Always offer a clear next step
- Provide phone number in every message
- Use Reply keywords (YES, URGENT, etc.) for easy response

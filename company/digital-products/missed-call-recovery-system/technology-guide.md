## Decision Framework

### Choose Weave if:
- You want everything in one system
- Budget is $400+/month
- You'll commit to 1-2 years

### Choose CallRail if:
- You want to test affordably
- You already have phone system
- You want quick setup

### Choose Solutionreach if:
- Patient communication is priority
- You want dental-specific features
- You need recall campaigns

### Choose Podium if:
- Reviews are main priority
- You want messaging + reviews
- You want easy mobile access

### Choose Manual if:
- Budget is tight
- You want to test concept first
- Call volume is low

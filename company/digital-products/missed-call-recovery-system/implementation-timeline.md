## Troubleshooting

### Low Response Rate
**Solutions:**
- Review template wording
- Add personalization
- Include specific questions
- Shorten messages

### Staff Not Following Process
**Solutions:**
- Simplify workflow
- Add reminders/notifications
- Provide more training
- Assign accountability

### Technology Issues
**Solutions:**
- Contact vendor support
- Check integrations
- Test with different phones
- Have backup manual process

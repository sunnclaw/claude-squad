# Missed Call Response Templates

## Why These Matter

Every missed call is potentially $300-$800 in lost revenue. These templates help you respond quickly and recover those opportunities.

---

## 1. New Patient Missed Call - Business Hours

**Use for:** Unknown number calling during business hours
**Channel:** SMS (send within 5 minutes)
**Goal:** Book an appointment

```
Hi! This is [PRACTICE NAME]. We missed your call but we'd love to help you with your dental needs.

Would you like to:
1. Schedule an appointment
2. Ask a question
3. Have us call you back

Just reply to this message, or call us back at [PHONE].

We're available [HOURS].
```

---

## 2. New Patient Missed Call - After Hours

**Use for:** Unknown number calling outside business hours
**Channel:** SMS
**Goal:** Acknowledge and set expectations

```
Hi! This is [PRACTICE NAME]. We received your call but our office is currently closed.

Our hours are: [HOURS]

We'll call you back first thing tomorrow morning, or you can book online anytime at [BOOKING LINK].

For dental emergencies, call [EMERGENCY LINE].

Thanks for reaching out!
```

---

## 3. Existing Patient Missed Call

**Use for:** Number matches patient records
**Channel:** SMS
**Goal:** Quick follow-up

```
Hi [PATIENT NAME], this is [PRACTICE NAME]. We noticed we missed your call today.

Is there something we can help you with? Just reply to this message or call us back at [PHONE].

We're here until [TIME] today.
```

---

## 4. Emergency Missed Call

**Use for:** Multiple calls in short period or emergency keywords detected
**Channel:** SMS + Priority callback
**Goal:** Immediate triage

```
[PRACTICE NAME] here - we see you tried to reach us multiple times. Are you experiencing a dental emergency?

If YES, call our emergency line now: [EMERGENCY NUMBER]

If you're in pain or have swelling, don't wait - call us immediately.

We're here to help.
```

---

## 5. Voicemail Left - Callback Confirmation

**Use for:** Patient left a voicemail
**Channel:** SMS
**Goal:** Acknowledge and set timeline

```
Hi [PATIENT NAME], we received your voicemail. A team member will call you back within [X] hours during business hours.

Need immediate assistance? Call [PHONE] or book online at [LINK].

Thanks!
[PRACTICE NAME]
```

---

## 6. Lunch Hour Missed Call

**Use for:** Calls during 12-1pm (typical lunch)
**Channel:** SMS
**Goal:** Set expectations

```
Hi! This is [PRACTICE NAME]. We're currently at lunch but will call you back within the hour.

Our afternoon hours: [HOURS]

If it's urgent, reply URGENT and we'll prioritize your callback.

Thanks for your patience!
```

---

## 7. Multiple Missed Calls - Follow Up

**Use for:** Patient called 2+ times without connecting
**Channel:** SMS + Priority callback
**Goal:** Urgent attention

```
Hi [PATIENT NAME], we see you've tried reaching us several times. We apologize for missing you!

A team member will call you within 30 minutes. If this is urgent, please reply URGENT or call our main line at [PHONE].

We value your call and want to help.
[PRACTICE NAME]
```

---

## 8. After-Hours Non-Emergency

**Use for:** Non-emergency calls after hours
**Channel:** SMS
**Goal:** Set expectations, prevent worry

```
Thanks for calling [PRACTICE NAME]! Our office is closed for the day, but we'll call you back when we open at [TIME] tomorrow.

Need to book? Visit [BOOKING LINK] anytime.

For dental emergencies only: [EMERGENCY NUMBER]

Good night!
```

## Response Time Targets

| Call Type | Target Response Time |
|-----------|---------------------|
| New patient (business hours) | Under 5 minutes |
| Existing patient | Under 10 minutes |
| Emergency | Immediate callback |
| After hours | Text immediately, call next business day |
| Voicemail | Within 2 hours during business hours |

---

## Best Practices

1. **Speed matters** - Every minute decreases conversion by 10%
2. **Personalize when possible** - Use names if you know them
3. **Provide alternatives** - Online booking, emergency numbers
4. **Set expectations** - When will you call back?
5. **Be proactive** - Don't wait for them to call back

---

## Customization Fields

- [PRACTICE NAME] - Your practice name
- [PHONE] - Main phone number
- [HOURS] - Current business hours
- [BOOKING LINK] - Online scheduling URL
- [EMERGENCY NUMBER] - After-hours emergency line
- [PATIENT NAME] - From caller ID lookup
- [TIME] - Specific time mentioned

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Automate the first response** - Let technology send the immediate text
2. **Prioritize callbacks** - New patients before existing
3. **Track patterns** - When do you miss the most calls?
4. **Staff accordingly** - Add coverage during peak missed call times
5. **Review weekly** - How many calls are you recovering?

# Appointment Confirmation Templates

## 1. New Patient Appointment Confirmation

**Use for:** First-time patients booking an appointment
**Channel:** SMS or Email
**Timing:** Immediately after booking

```
Hi [PATIENT NAME]!

Welcome to [PRACTICE NAME]! We're excited to meet you.

Your appointment is confirmed:
📅 Date: [DATE]
🕐 Time: [TIME]
📍 Address: [ADDRESS]
📞 Phone: [PHONE]

Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete new patient paperwork. Bring:
• Photo ID
• Insurance card
• List of current medications

Questions? Reply to this message or call us at [PHONE].

See you soon!
The [PRACTICE NAME] Team
```

## 5. Procedure Appointment Confirmation

**Use for:** Major procedures (root canal, extraction, crown, etc.)
**Channel:** Email (preferred) or SMS
**Timing:** Day of booking + 2 days before

```
Hi [PATIENT NAME],

Your procedure appointment is confirmed:

📅 Procedure: [PROCEDURE NAME]
📅 Date: [DATE]
🕐 Time: [TIME]
⏱️ Duration: Approximately [DURATION]
📍 [ADDRESS]

BEFORE YOUR APPOINTMENT:
• Eat a light meal before arriving
• Take any prescribed medications as directed
• Arrange for a ride home (sedation patients)

WHAT TO BRING:
• Insurance card
• Photo ID
• Any prescribed medications

AFTER YOUR APPOINTMENT:
• Plan to rest for the remainder of the day
• Soft foods recommended for [X] hours/days
• Avoid strenuous activity for 24 hours

Questions? Call us at [PHONE].

See you soon,
[PRACTICE NAME]
```

---

## Best Practices

1. **Send immediately** - Confirm appointments as soon as they're booked
2. **Include essentials** - Date, time, location, contact info
3. **Add value** - Include preparation instructions or what to bring
4. **Clear CTA** - Tell them what to do if they need to change anything
5. **Brand consistency** - Use your practice name and maintain a warm, professional tone

---

## Customization Tips

Replace bracketed fields:
- [PATIENT NAME] - First name preferred
- [DATE] - Full date (e.g., "Monday, March 15")
- [TIME] - Include AM/PM
- [ADDRESS] - Full address or just city if local
- [PHONE] - Main practice line
- [DURATION] - Expected appointment length
- [PROCEDURE NAME] - Specific treatment name

## The Implementation

Dr. Smith implemented our recommendations over one weekend:

### Day 1 (Saturday): Technology Setup
- Enabled auto-text response on her phone system
- Set up online scheduling link
- Configured missed call notifications to her cell

### Day 2 (Sunday): Team Training
- 30-minute meeting with front desk staff
- Practiced the 3-ring rule and greeting scripts
- Set up staggered lunch coverage schedule

### Day 3 (Monday): Launch
- New system went live
- Tracked every call, text, and appointment

## Week 1 Results

| Day | Missed Calls | Text Responses | Appointments Booked |

1. **82% response rate** - Up from 22% with voicemail alone

4. **Staff morale improved** - Less stress, better patient interactions

## The Phone Skills That Matter

### 1. The 3-Ring Rule

Answer every call within 3 rings. Why?

- Ring 1: Professional (you're on top of things)
- Ring 2: Still acceptable
- Ring 3: Patient is getting impatient
- Ring 4+: They're looking at other options

**Training tip:** Have staff count rings out loud during practice sessions.

### 2. The Warm Greeting Script

```
"Good [morning/afternoon]! Thank you for calling [Practice Name].
This is [Name]. How can I help you today?"
```

Avoid: "Hello?" or "[Practice Name], hold please."

### 3. The Call Control Technique

Guide the conversation:

- Ask open-ended questions first
- Then narrow down with specific options
- Always offer appointment times, never ask "When would you like to come in?"

**Bad:** "When works for you?"
**Good:** "We have openings Tuesday at 10am or Thursday at 2pm. Which works better?"

### 4. The Objection Handlers

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "How much does it cost?" | "That depends on your specific needs. Dr. [Name] offers a free consultation where we can give you an exact quote. Would Tuesday at 10am work?" |
| "I need to check my schedule" | "I understand! While I have you on the phone, let me put a tentative hold on [time]. If you need to change, just give us a call." |
| "I'm shopping around" | "That's smart! We actually have a price-match guarantee. What have you been quoted so far?" |

## Reducing Missed Calls Through Teamwork

### The Buddy System

When one staff member is:
- On the phone → Another watches for incoming calls
- With a patient → Desk is still covered
- At lunch → Phones are forwarded or staffed

### The Lunch Hour Solution

The 12pm-1pm slot is the #1 missed call window. Solutions:

1. **Staggered lunches** - Half the staff eats at 11:30, half at 12:30
2. **Phone forwarding** - Forward to a cell phone during lunch
3. **Virtual receptionist** - Service handles calls during breaks

### The After-Hours Protocol

When the office closes:

1. Update voicemail with next-day availability
2. Set up text-back auto-reply
3. Have an on-call number for emergencies
4. Check messages first thing in the morning

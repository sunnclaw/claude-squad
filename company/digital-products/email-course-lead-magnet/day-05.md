## The 24/7 Phone Technology Stack

### Layer 1: Smart Phone System

Modern dental practices use cloud-based phone systems that include:

- **Call routing** - Direct calls to the right person
- **Missed call alerts** - Instant notifications via text/email
- **Call recording** - Review for training and quality
- **Analytics** - Track call volume, peak times, answer rates

**Recommended:** RingCentral, Grasshopper, or Dialpad

### Layer 2: Auto-Responder

When calls go unanswered, technology takes over:

```
Automatic text sent within 30 seconds:
"Thanks for calling [Practice]! We missed you but are here to help.
Reply to schedule or ask questions. We'll respond within 15 minutes
during business hours."
```

### Layer 3: Online Scheduling

Let patients book while you're busy or closed:

- Embed on your website
- Available 24/7
- Syncs with your practice software
- Sends automatic confirmations

**Recommended:** Calendly, Acuity, or integrated with your practice software

### Layer 4: Virtual Receptionist

For after-hours and overflow:

- AI-powered or live agents
- Books appointments directly
- Handles emergencies appropriately
- Forwards urgent calls to on-call staff

**ROI:** One saved emergency appointment pays for a month of service

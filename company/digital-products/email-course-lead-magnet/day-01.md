**Subject Line:** 📞 Your practice is losing $47,000/year (and you don't know it)

---

Dear Dental Practice Owner,

Did you know the average dental practice misses 15-20% of incoming calls? That's not just a statistic - it's real money walking out your door.

## The Real Cost Calculator

Let's break down what each missed call actually costs you:

| Metric | Your Practice |
|--------|---------------|
| Average new patient value | $1,200 |
| Calls missed per day | 3 |
| Days open per year | 250 |
| New patient conversion rate | 40% |

**Annual Revenue Lost: 3 calls × 250 days × 40% × $1,200 = $360,000**

Even if your numbers are half of this example, you're looking at **$180,000 in potential revenue** simply vanishing because nobody picked up the phone.

## Where Do Missed Calls Come From?

- **Lunch hours** - Staff is eating, phones ring out
- **Patient care** - Hygienists and assistants can't answer
- **After hours** - No voicemail callback system
- **High volume periods** - Multiple lines ringing at once
- **Staff shortages** - One person can't handle everything

## The Invisible Profit Leak

Most practice owners don't realize this is happening because:

1. You don't see the calls you never answered
2. Patients don't leave voicemails anymore (they just call your competitor)
3. Your phone system doesn't report missed calls prominently

Dear Dental Practice Owner,

What if you could recover more than half of your missed calls with a simple text message?

## The Strategy That Changes Everything

Here's the exact text-back strategy that top-performing practices use:

### Step 1: Set Up Auto-Reply (Immediate)

When a call goes unanswered, automatically send:

```
"Hi! Thanks for calling [Practice Name]. We missed your call but want to help.
Reply with:
1 - Schedule appointment
2 - Ask a question
3 - Emergency/urgent

We'll respond within 15 minutes. - Dr. [Name]'s Office"
```

### Step 2: The Follow-Up (Within 15 Minutes)

Send a personal follow-up:

```
"Hi [Name], this is [Staff Name] from [Practice]. I saw you called earlier.
How can I help you today? We have openings this [day] at [time]."
```

### Step 3: The Conversion

When they respond, move fast:

```
"Great! I have you scheduled for [day] at [time] with Dr. [Name].
You'll receive a confirmation text shortly. Any questions, just reply here!"
```

## Why This Works

| Traditional Voicemail | Text-Back Strategy |
|----------------------|-------------------|
| 20% callback rate | 60%+ response rate |
| Patient waits passively | Patient engages actively |
| One-way communication | Two-way conversation |
| No data captured | Phone number captured |
| Feels impersonal | Feels personal |

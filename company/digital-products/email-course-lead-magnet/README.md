# 7-Day Email Course: Never Lose a Patient to a Missed Call

A lead magnet email course for dental practice decision-makers.

## Course Overview

This 7-day email course teaches dental practices how to recover revenue from missed calls. It serves as a lead magnet to capture email addresses and nurture prospects.

## Structure

- **Day 1:** The Hidden Cost of Missed Calls (ROI Calculator)
- **Day 2:** Why Voicemail is Killing Your Practice
- **Day 3:** The 5-Minute Text-Back Strategy
- **Day 4:** Training Your Front Desk for Success
- **Day 5:** Technology That Works While You Sleep
- **Day 6:** Case Study: How Dr. Smith Recovered 12 Appointments
- **Day 7:** Your Implementation Action Plan

## Setup Instructions

### Email Service Integration

1. Copy the email content from each day's markdown file
2. Set up in your email service provider (ConvertKit, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, etc.)
3. Configure as an automated sequence with 1-day intervals
4. Add lead capture form to your landing page

### Recommended Email Service Providers

- **ConvertKit** - Best for creators, easy automation
- **Mailchimp** - Free tier available, user-friendly
- **ActiveCampaign** - Advanced automation features
- **SendGrid** - Good for transactional emails

### Lead Capture Setup

1. Create a landing page with the course offer
2. Add a signup form connected to your email list
3. Configure double opt-in for deliverability
4. Set up the automated sequence trigger

## Customization

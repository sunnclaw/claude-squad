# Day 7: Your Implementation Action Plan

**Subject Line:** ✅ Your 7-day transformation plan (let's do this!)

---

Dear Dental Practice Owner,

You've learned the strategies. Now let's put them into action.

This is your step-by-step implementation plan to transform your phone system and recover thousands in lost revenue.

## Your 7-Day Implementation Plan

### Day 1: Assessment & Goals

**Morning (30 minutes)**
- [ ] Pull phone reports for the last 30 days
- [ ] Count total missed calls
- [ ] Count voicemails left (typically 20% of missed calls)
- [ ] Calculate your revenue loss: Missed calls × 0.55 × $1,200

**Afternoon (30 minutes)**
- [ ] Set your target: What % of missed calls do you want to recover?
- [ ] Identify your biggest missed call windows (lunch, after-hours, busy periods)
- [ ] Schedule a team meeting for Day 2

### Day 2: Technology Selection

**Morning (1 hour)**
- [ ] Research phone systems with text-back capability
- [ ] Check if your current system has this feature (many do!)
- [ ] Compare: RingCentral, Grasshopper, Dialpad, Weave

**Afternoon (1 hour)**
- [ ] Choose your technology stack (start simple!)
- [ ] Sign up for free trials if available
- [ ] Schedule installation/setup call with provider

### Day 3: System Setup

**Full Day Focus**
- [ ] Configure auto-text response message
- [ ] Set up missed call notifications
- [ ] Enable online scheduling (if applicable)
- [ ] Test the entire flow yourself (call your office and hang up)

**Sample Auto-Text Template:**
```
"Thanks for calling [Practice Name]! We missed you but want to help.
Reply with:
1 - Schedule an appointment
2 - Ask a question
3 - Urgent dental issue

We'll respond within 15 minutes during business hours."
```

### Day 4: Team Training

**Morning Meeting (45 minutes)**
- [ ] Explain the new system to staff
- [ ] Practice the 3-ring rule
- [ ] Role-play greeting scripts
- [ ] Review objection handlers

**Afternoon Practice (30 minutes)**
- [ ] Have staff call the office and test responses
- [ ] Practice text-back conversations
- [ ] Set up staggered lunch coverage

### Day 5-6: Soft Launch

**Monitor and Adjust**
- [ ] Track every call, text, and appointment
- [ ] Note any issues or questions from staff
- [ ] Adjust auto-text message if needed
- [ ] Celebrate wins with the team

### Day 7: Full Launch + Review

**Morning**
- [ ] Final team check-in
- [ ] Review first 5 days of data
- [ ] Identify what's working and what needs adjustment

**Afternoon**
- [ ] Set weekly review schedule
- [ ] Create a simple tracking dashboard
- [ ] Plan next improvements

## Success Metrics to Track

| Metric | Week 1 Target | Month 1 Target |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| Response rate | 50%+ | 70%+ |
| Appointments from missed calls | 20%+ | 40%+ |
| Callback time | < 30 min | < 15 min |
| Patient satisfaction | Ask! | Ask! |

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Trying to do too much at once** - Start with text-back, add features later
❌ **Skipping team training** - Technology only works if people use it
❌ **Not testing yourself** - Call your own office regularly
❌ **Ignoring data** - What gets measured gets improved
❌ **Set it and forget it** - Review weekly for best results

## Your Quick-Start Checklist

If you want to start TODAY, do this:

- [ ] Enable missed call notifications on your phone (5 min)
- [ ] Create a simple auto-text response (10 min)
- [ ] Brief your front desk on the 3-ring rule (5 min)
- [ ] Stagger lunch breaks starting tomorrow (5 min)
- [ ] Schedule next week's review meeting (2 min)

**Total time to start: 27 minutes**

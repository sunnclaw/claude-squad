## Slide 10: FAQ - Common Objections

## "We already have voicemail"

Voicemail is passive. Studies show 80% of callers who reach voicemail hang up and call the next practice. Instant text keeps them engaged.

## "We don't have budget"

The pilot is designed to pay for itself. If you recover just 2-3 appointments (each worth $200-500), the pilot is covered.

## "Our front desk handles calls fine"

Even the best front desk can't answer every call during patient check-in, lunch, or emergencies. This catches what slips through.

## "We're not technical"

Your team just returns calls from the queue. No software training required.

## "We've tried call tracking before"

Most call tracking tools are built for marketing attribution, not recovery. We focus on the operational gap: what happens AFTER the missed call.

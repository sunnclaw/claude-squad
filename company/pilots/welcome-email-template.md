## Email Sending Guidelines

1. **Personalization:** Always use the customer's actual name and clinic name
2. **Timing:** Send within 2 hours of account creation
3. **Subject Line:** Test different options (Option 1 recommended for new customers)
4. **Attachments:** Include all three documents in PDF format
5. **Tracking:** Log the email send in the CRM
6. **Follow-up:** If no response within 24 hours, send a follow-up

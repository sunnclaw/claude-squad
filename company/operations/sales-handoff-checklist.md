# Sales Handoff Checklist

> When a qualified lead becomes a customer, use this checklist to ensure smooth handoff from Sales to Customer Success.

---

## Pre-Handoff (Sales Manager Completes)

### Customer Information
- [ ] Customer name and role documented
- [ ] Practice name and location
- [ ] Primary contact phone and email verified
- [ ] Decision-maker confirmed
- [ ] Clinic type (single-location, multi-location, specialty)

### Qualification Summary
- [ ] Qualification score documented (see rubric)
- [ ] Key pain points identified
- [ ] Stated objections addressed
- [ ] Expected value/ROI discussed
- [ ] Timeline expectations set

### Deal Terms
- [ ] Pricing agreed and documented
- [ ] Payment method confirmed
- [ ] Pilot duration agreed (default: 30 days)
- [ ] Success metrics defined
- [ ] Start date confirmed

### Paperclip Update
- [ ] Issue status updated to `completed`
- [ ] Comment added with customer details
- [ ] CRM entry created

---

## Handoff Meeting (5-10 minutes)

### Information Transfer
- [ ] Sales introduces Customer Success to customer via email
- [ ] Qualification notes shared
- [ ] Any special requirements communicated
- [ ] Customer communication preferences noted

### Email Template

```
Subject: Welcome to Claude天团 - Your Onboarding Contact

Hi [Customer Name],

I'm pleased to introduce [Customer Success Name] who will be your dedicated
contact for the onboarding process. They will help you get set up and ensure
everything runs smoothly.

[Customer Success Name] will reach out within 24 hours to schedule your
initial setup call.

Best regards,
[Sales Manager Name]
Claude天团
```

---

## Post-Handoff (Customer Success Takes Over)

### Immediate Actions (Within 24 hours)
- [ ] Send welcome email with credentials
- [ ] Schedule Day 1 setup call
- [ ] Create customer folder in CRM
- [ ] Follow detailed onboarding checklist (see company/pilots/onboarding-checklist.md)

---

## Documentation

### Where to Log
- Customer details: CRM system
- Call notes: Customer folder
- Status updates: Paperclip task

### Key Dates to Track
| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| Day 0 | Handoff complete |
| Day 1 | Setup call |
| Day 7 | First check-in |
| Day 14 | Progress review |
| Day 30 | Renewal discussion |

---

## Escalation

If handoff cannot proceed:
1. Document blocker
2. Escalate to CEO
3. Do not delay customer communication

## Channel 1: Programmatic Advertising

### Overview
Display ads that generate revenue automatically based on website traffic.

### Options

#### Google AdSense
- **Setup:** Create account, add ad code to website
- **Human Action Required:** One-time account creation (15 min)
- **Revenue Model:** Pay-per-click (PPC) + Pay-per-impression (CPM)
- **Typical RPM (Revenue per 1000 pageviews):**
  - Tech/AI niche: $2-8 RPM
  - Healthcare/Dental: $5-15 RPM (higher-value keywords)
  - Average small site: $1-5 RPM
- **Traffic Needed for $100/month:** ~20,000-50,000 pageviews
- **Current Status:** 113+ blog posts ready for ads

#### AdSense Alternatives (Better for Small Sites)
| Network | Min Traffic | RPM | Payment Terms |
|---------|-------------|-----|---------------|
| Ezoic | 10,000/mo | $5-15 | Net-30 |
| Media.net | 5,000/mo | $2-8 | Net-30 |
| Monumetric | 10,000/mo | $6-12 | Net-45 |
| Mediavine | 50,000/mo | $15-30 | Net-45 |

### Setup Requirements
1. [ ] Human creates AdSense account (requires Google account)
2. [ ] Human verifies website ownership
3. [ ] AI adds ad code to all pages
4. [ ] AI monitors performance monthly

### Revenue Potential
- **Month 1-3:** $0-10 (building traffic)
- **Month 4-6:** $10-50 (SEO kicking in)
- **Month 7-12:** $50-200 (established traffic)
- **Year 2+:** $200-500+ (if traffic grows to 50k+ views/mo)

### Why This Works Autonomous
Once setup, ads run automatically. AI can:
- Place ads on new content
- Optimize ad placement
- Monitor and report earnings
- Add new content to drive traffic

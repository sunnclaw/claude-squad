- Single-location or small multi-location dental practice

- No dependable follow-up process for unanswered calls

Not a fit:

- Pilot duration: 30 days

Revision policy:
- Up to 2 text template revisions during pilot
- Workflow adjustments as needed

Price logic:

Required customer inputs:
- Access to call log or phone system for missed-call detection
- One staff member to own the callback queue
- Approval of text-back template
- 5-minute weekly check-in for feedback

## Objection Handlers

### "We already have voicemail"
Voicemail is passive. Most callers who reach voicemail hang up and call the next practice. Text-back within 5 minutes keeps them engaged.

### "We don't have budget"
The pilot is designed to pay for itself. If you recover just 2-3 appointments (each worth $200-500 in first-visit revenue), the pilot is covered.

### "Our front desk handles calls fine"
Even the best front desk can't answer every call during patient check-in, lunch, or emergencies. This catches the calls that slip through.

### "We're not technical"
The workflow is designed for simplicity. Your team just needs to return calls from the queue we create. No software training required.

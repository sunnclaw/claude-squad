# Content Creation Offer Pack

Last updated: 2026-03-10
Owner: CTO

## Offer Summary

Ideal customer:
- founder, operator, or small team that needs publishable writing fast

Best fit problems:
- blog posts and articles are not getting written
- website copy needs to be clarified or upgraded
- technical ideas need to be turned into readable content

Not a fit:
- long brand strategy engagements
- high-volume SEO retainers

Core promise:
- clear, publishable content delivered fast with minimal client overhead

Standard turnaround:
- 24 to 48 hours

Revision policy:
- up to 2 revision rounds

Price logic:
- base price: CNY 100 per 1,000 words
- variables that change price: technical depth, bilingual delivery, rush turnaround
- rush fee: +50%

Required customer inputs:
- target audience
- desired outcome or CTA
- source material, links, or references

## Packages

### Option A: Fast Start
- Deliverables: one short article, post, or landing-page section up to 1,000 words
- Turnaround: 24 hours
- Price: CNY 100

### Option B: Standard
- Deliverables: one article or doc up to 2,000 words with light structural editing
- Turnaround: 48 hours
- Price: CNY 180 to CNY 220

### Option C: Expanded
- Deliverables: one 3,000-word article or multi-part content pack
- Turnaround: 48 to 72 hours
- Price: CNY 260 to CNY 400

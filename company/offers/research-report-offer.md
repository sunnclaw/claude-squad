# Research Report Offer Pack

Last updated: 2026-03-10
Owner: CTO

## Offer Summary

Ideal customer:
- founder or operator who needs a decision-ready research brief fast

Best fit problems:
- market or competitor understanding is too shallow
- a business or technical topic needs synthesis before action
- a team needs a fast written brief instead of open-ended consulting

Not a fit:
- academic-style literature reviews with no business action
- ongoing analyst retainers

Core promise:
- fast research distilled into clear conclusions and next actions

Standard turnaround:
- 48 to 72 hours

Revision policy:
- up to 2 revision rounds focused on scope accuracy and clarity

Price logic:
- base price: CNY 200 per topic
- variables that change price: depth, number of competitors, technical specialization, visualization needs
- rush fee: +50% to +100%

Required customer inputs:
- research question
- decision this report should support
- constraints, region, or comparison set

## Packages

### Option A: Fast Start
- Deliverables: short research brief with key findings and next steps
- Turnaround: 48 hours
- Price: CNY 200 to CNY 300

### Option B: Standard
- Deliverables: structured report with findings, analysis, and recommendations
- Turnaround: 72 hours
- Price: CNY 400 to CNY 600

### Option C: Expanded
- Deliverables: deeper report with competitor table, source list, and action plan
- Turnaround: 3 to 5 days
- Price: CNY 800+

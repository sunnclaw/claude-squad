## The Problem

When your front desk is busy, at lunch, or after hours, inbound calls go unanswered. Each missed call is a potential new patient who may book elsewhere if they don't hear back fast.

## Our Solution

A lightweight 30-day pilot that recovers missed-call revenue without adding headcount:

- **Instant text-back** to every missed caller
- **Prioritized callback queue** owned by someone on your team
- **Weekly recovery report** showing appointments saved

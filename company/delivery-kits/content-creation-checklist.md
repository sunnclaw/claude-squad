# Content Creation Delivery Kit

Last updated: 2026-03-10
Owner: CTO

## Purpose

Start and finish content work from a repeatable checklist so delivery stays fast and quality stays predictable.

## Definition Of Ready

Do not start until these are clear:
- order ID
- package and word-count range
- target audience
- intended outcome or CTA
- source material or explicit note that none was provided
- payment state
- deadline

## Start Checklist

- confirm the quote matches the requested deliverable
- confirm tone, language, and channel
- collect links, references, or examples
- restate what is included and what is out of scope
- open the order row in the dashboard

## Draft Checklist

- write to the requested audience, not a generic reader
- make the opening state the core problem or promise
- keep the structure scannable with clear sections
- verify any factual claims that could damage trust
- end with the requested CTA or next step

## QA Checklist

- title and opening are specific
- the draft answers the brief directly
- the tone matches the requested style
- repetition and filler are removed
- grammar and formatting are clean
- the file name is clear for delivery

## Delivery Package

Send:
- final content file
- short note describing what was delivered
- revision window and scope reminder

## Internal Reuse Capture

After delivery, record at least one:
- phrase or angle that improved conversion
- scoping issue to add to the offer pack
- reusable prompt or brief structure
- before/after example for proof content
